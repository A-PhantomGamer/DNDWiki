Key Locations

Neverwinter

Major City

Known as the City of Skilled Hands or the Jewel of the North, Neverwinter is a bustling city on the Sword Coast. Despite being devastated by the eruption of Mount Hotenow, the city is rebuilding under Lord Neverember's leadership.

Population: ~23,000

Government: Lordship (Lord Dagult Neverember)

Notable Features: Castle Never, Hall of Justice, Neverwinter River

View Full Details

Phandalin

Frontier Settlement

A small mining town that was abandoned and has recently been resettled by hardy frontiersmen seeking to make their fortunes in the nearby mines. The town has been troubled by bandits known as the Redbrands.

Population: ~150

Government: Town Master (Harbin Wester)

Notable Features: Stonehill Inn, Townmaster's Hall, Tresendar Manor

View Full Details

Wave Echo Cave

Ancient Mine (Secret)

The legendary Wave Echo Cave contains the Forge of Spells, a magical location where dwarves and gnomes once crafted powerful magic items. The cave was lost for centuries after a devastating orc attack.

Status: Rediscovered

Current Occupants: Various monsters, Black Spider's forces

Notable Features: Forge of Spells, underground river, ancient dwarven halls

"Security through strength."

Leader: Council of Lords

Calendar & Time

The Calendar of Harptos

The Faerûnian calendar consists of twelve months of thirty days each, with five special holidays that fall between certain months.

Hammer (Deepwinter)

The first month of the year, during the depths of winter.

Current campaign date: Hammer 15, 1491 DR

Midwinter Holiday

A special holiday between Hammer and Alturiak.
